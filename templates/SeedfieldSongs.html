What's here

You can see the songs we've sung by Date, by Song name or by when the song was New, just click on the links below:

<%def name="headerLink(text,href)">   ${text} % for linktext in linksOnHomepage.keys(): ${headerLink(linktext,links[linksOnHomepage[linktext]])} % endfor

When you see a number under "In the last", if you move your mouse over it or tap it, you'll get a list of when it's been used. The pages are quite slow to load because there's a lots of data on each page.

These pages were created on ${today.isoformat()}. Ask Andrew to update the pages if they're out of date!
